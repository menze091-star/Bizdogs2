Who We Are

More Than a Dorm. A Community.

BizDogs brings together first-year students in the Labovitz School of Business and Economics (LSBE) who share a floor in Lake Superior Hall and a drive to get ahead — academically, professionally, and personally.

Professional Development

Build Your Network Early

From resume workshops to mock interviews and live networking mixers with LSBE alumni and local employers — BizDogs get a head start that most students don't find until junior year.

Site Visits

Real Business, Real Places

Step inside organizations like Essentia Health and see business theory come to life. Our site visits connect classroom learning to the industries shaping Duluth and beyond.

Community Service

Give Back to Duluth

BizDogs partner with local nonprofits like CHUM to serve the Duluth community. Building empathy and civic responsibility is part of becoming a complete business leader.

Socials

Find Your People

Late-night study sessions, team trivia, and outdoor adventures — living alongside your classmates turns acquaintances into lifelong friends and collaborators.

MBA 4+1 Collaboration

Get Connected to Graduate Students

BizDogs get exclusive access to mentor relationships with students in UMD's MBA 4+1 program — a unique pipeline that gives you a five-year view of your business education from the very first semester.

Student Leadership

Meet the BizDogs Council

The BizDogs Council is elected by the community to plan events, represent members, and make sure your first year is unforgettable.

Sam Ashby

President

Sam leads the BizDogs Council with energy and vision, driving programming that connects members to professional growth opportunities and campus life. As president, Sam ensures every BizDog feels welcome and empowered from move-in day onward.

Caleb Cooley

Vice President

Caleb works alongside the president to coordinate events, manage council operations, and build bridges between BizDogs and the wider LSBE community. His organizational skills keep the community running smoothly all year long.

Madi Welborn

Ready to Run With Us?

Join BizDogs

BizDogs is designed for incoming first-year students admitted to the Labovitz School of Business and Economics at UMD. Here's what you need to know:

🏠

Live in Lake Superior Hall

BizDogs live together on a dedicated floor in Lake Superior Hall — one of UMD's most connected residence halls — putting you steps from your cohort 24/7.

📚

Take Classes Together

BizDogs are co-enrolled in select first-year LSBE courses, so you show up to class already knowing people. Built-in study groups, built-in accountability.

🧑‍💼

Exclusive Programming

Access professional development events, site visits, networking opportunities, and social activities that are reserved exclusively for BizDogs members.

🎓

Head Start on Your Career

Graduate-level connections, industry exposure, and a professional mindset — all in your first year. BizDogs alumni consistently stand out in internship and job searches.

How to Join

  1. 01
    Apply to UMD & LSBE

    You must be admitted to the University of Minnesota Duluth and declare a pre-business or LSBE major as your intended program of study.

  2. 02
    Select Lake Superior Hall

    When completing your housing application, select Lake Superior Hall and indicate your interest in the BizDogs LLC.

  3. 03
    Get Confirmed

    UMD Housing and LSBE will confirm your placement in the BizDogs community. You'll receive more details via your UMD email before move-in.

  4. 04
    Show Up & Get Involved

    Attend Welcome Week BizDogs orientation, meet your council, and hit the ground running from day one of your first year.
